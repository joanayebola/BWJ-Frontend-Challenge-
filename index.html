Frontend Developer Challenges

Welcome to the Frontend Developer Challenge hosted by BWJ Community. This challenge series is designed to help you sharpen your frontend development skills, regardless of your current expertise. Our goal is to provide a platform where developers at all levels can enhance their abilities and showcase their talent. These challenges offer real-world scenarios and projects to work on, allowing you to learn and grow in a supportive community. We encourage you to participate, learn from others, and ultimately create a positive impact through your web development skills. We look forward to seeing your creative solutions to these challenges.

If you have any questions or issues, feel free to reach out to BWJCommunity on Twitter.

Challenge 1: Beginner

Task: Create a simple personal portfolio webpage for "Laura James" Include a title, introduction, image, and navigation links.

Submission:

  • Upload your code to GitHub and create a repository named "BWJchallenge1."
  • Share the live link on Twitter with the hashtag #BWJCommunity. You can also tag BWJCommunity and codesbyjoan in your tweet
